Welcome to online Exam system

An online examination system is a digital platform that streamlines the creation, delivery, and management of assessments, moving away from traditional paper-based tests to offer greater efficiency, security, and accessibility for institutions and students. These systems automate processes like question bank management, test scheduling, and grading (especially for objective questions), providing instant results and reducing administrative burdens, while offering features like secure browsers and webcam monitoring to maintain integrity. They enable remote testing on various devices, cut costs, and provide valuable data, making them a modern, scalable solution for education and corporate training.
